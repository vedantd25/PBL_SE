About Us

The primary purpose of a network of NGOs is to foster collaboration, coordination, and knowledge exchange among its member organizations. By sharing expertise, best practices, and resources, these networks aim to address complex social, environmental, or humanitarian challenges more effectively. Networks of NGOs often focus on specific thematic areas or sectors, such as human rights, healthcare, education, environmental conservation, or poverty alleviation. They serve as platforms for NGOs to collectively advocate for policy changes, influence decision-making processes, and engage in joint initiatives or campaigns. Here is where we come in. DEEPASTAMBH does a noble job of connecting to different NGOs and fulfill the need with collective contribution.

Why the need of this website

The need for a website connecting NGOs, volunteers, and donors arises from the desire to overcome challenges in the social sector and maximize impact. This platform serves as a centralized hub, providing easy access to information about NGOs and their initiatives, enabling individuals and organizations to find and support causes aligned with their interests. It facilitates engagement by matching volunteers with suitable opportunities and encourages collaboration among NGOs, fostering effectiveness and sustainability. Additionally, the platform's donation portal ensures transparency and trust, allowing donors to contribute securely and witness the impact of their support. In summary, the website addresses the need for accessibility, engagement, and funding within the social sector. By connecting NGOs, volunteers, and donors, it enhances the sector's reach, facilitates meaningful collaborations, and empowers individuals and organizations to create positive change.

Our Journey

Our journey began with a vision to connect NGOs, volunteers, and donors on a comprehensive online platform. We conducted extensive research and assembled a dedicated team to build a user-friendly website that would empower the social sector. Our platform features an NGO directory, allowing users to easily find and support NGOs aligned with their interests. We facilitate volunteer matching, enabling individuals to discover meaningful opportunities and contribute their skills. Through our secure donation portal, users can make a direct impact by supporting causes they believe in. We promote knowledge sharing and capacity building among NGOs through a resource library. Transparency and trust are key, as we provide a platform for user feedback and ratings. We remain committed to empowering the social sector and fostering collaboration to drive positive change.

Our aim

  • Accessibility: To make information about NGOs easily accessible to users, enabling them to find relevant NGOs based on their geographical location, focus areas, and services provided. The website should provide a user-friendly interface that allows people to search, filter, and navigate through the directory of NGOs effortlessly.
  • Connection and Engagement: To facilitate connections between NGOs and individuals who are interested in volunteering their time, skills, or resources. The website should provide a platform for NGOs to showcase their work and initiatives, while allowing users to express their interest, contact NGOs, and participate in their activities.
  • Support and Empowerment: To empower NGOs by providing them with an online presence and visibility. The website should offer dedicated profiles or webpages for each NGO, allowing them to share their mission, projects, impact stories, and donation requirements. This exposure can help NGOs attract volunteers, donors, and collaboration opportunities, ultimately supporting their sustainability and growth.
  • Resource Sharing: To foster knowledge sharing and collaboration among NGOs. The website should offer a platform where NGOs can share resources, best practices, and success stories, helping them learn from each other and enhance their effectiveness. This initiative can contribute to the overall development and improvement of the NGO sector.
  • Transparency and Trust: To promote transparency within the NGO sector by providing user feedback and ratings. The website should allow users to provide reviews and ratings based on their experiences with NGOs, helping build trust and accountability. This feedback mechanism can assist users in making informed decisions about supporting and engaging with NGOs.
