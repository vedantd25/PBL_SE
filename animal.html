Animal Rahat

Animal Rahat was founded in 2003 to make a difference in the lives of working bullocks, donkeys, ponies, horses, and other animals in the Indian states of Maharashtra and Uttar Pradesh. Veterinary teams at four regional offices help thousands of animals in often desperate need, while the group’s two sanctuaries provide rescued and retired animals with a lifetime of care.

Wildlife SOS

Wildlife SOS was established to make lasting changes to protect and conserve India’s natural heritage, forests and biodiversity. We actively work towards protecting wildlife, conserving habitat, studying biodiversity, conducting research, and creating alternative and sustainable livelihoods for erstwhile communities that depend on wildlife for sustenance.

Help in Suffering

We give medical treatment to dogs, cats, equines, camels, monkey, birds and more from our hospital compound located in Jaipur Rajasthan. Established in 1980, the Trust treats many thousands of street animals every year. The hospital and shelter was founded by Miss Crystal Rogers aided by Mr. G.K. Tiwari and (late) Dr. T. Ramchandani.
